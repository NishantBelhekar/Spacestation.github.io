About us

Vyom Space Station is a Space Station theme center/park. Located in Mumbai,Maharashtra, India. The motive of this theme park is to aware people about space, give information on it in a easy and entartaining way. Vyom Space Station was established in 2012. It is founded by ISRO Ex-chairman VK Naidu and Amar Singh(Businessman). We had collaborated with famous institution like ISRO, NASA, JAXA, ROSCOMOS, ESA for Astronaut Training and Research. In our park we have big telescope for Space Hunt, Anti-Gravity Space, Moon Surface Walk Room, Mars Surface Walk Room and many more.
